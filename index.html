NaijaTax Calculator

How It Works

Income / Corporate Tax

Enter your salary or company profit to calculate PAYE for individuals or corporate tax for businesses, including small, standard, and multinational companies.

VAT Calculator

Input the price of goods or services to calculate VAT (7.5%). You can mark items as VAT-exempt for accurate totals.

Withholding Tax (WHT)

Calculate tax deducted at source on payments like rent, dividends, interest, or professional fees with different rates (5%, 10%, 15%).

Capital Gains Tax (CGT)

Compute tax on profits from selling assets or property. Rates: 10% for individuals and 30% for companies.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select your taxpayer type: individual, small company, standard company, or multinational.
  2. Enter your annual income or company profit.
  3. Input VAT items, WHT payments, or assets sold for CGT.
  4. Click "Calculate All Taxes" to get a complete breakdown.
  5. View each tax component with notes and totals for clarity.
